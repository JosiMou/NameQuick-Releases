Release date: 2026-05-19

New

Improvements

  • Made credit updates more reliable for paid plans. NameQuick now handles subscription credit renewals more consistently, especially when account details arrive in stages.
  • Improved credit status during account setup. Trial and paid-plan setup now keep showing the right setup state while NameQuick finishes checking your account.
  • Made saved templates more reliable. Templates and custom prompts are better protected during settings resets, so saved naming workflows should remain available.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed trial setup showing the wrong credit state. Trial activation should now stay in the correct setup flow instead of briefly showing unrelated credit or license messaging.
  • Fixed paid credits sometimes appearing unavailable after renewal. NameQuick now keeps checking the right account information before deciding whether credits can be used.
  • Fixed templates and custom prompts disappearing after a reset. Saved naming templates and custom prompts should now remain available instead of unexpectedly resetting.
