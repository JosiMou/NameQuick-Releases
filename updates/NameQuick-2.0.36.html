NameQuick 2.0.36

  • Drag to reorder folders in the sidebar
  • Exclude Subfolders now sticks reliably
  • Re-adding removed folders works immediately (no extra permission steps)
  • Better filename fidelity and control
    • Parentheses and square brackets are now preserved in names (e.g., “(Company)”, “[25 AUG ’25]”).
    • Default cleanup only removes macOS‑illegal characters; your custom prompt’s formatting is kept intact.
    • “Clean filenames” remains optional for stricter ASCII/transliteration, if you need it.
  • General stability improvements
