NameQuick 2.1.0

A fresh start. This release reimagines how you get started with NameQuick and makes the whole experience feel more polished and intuitive.

New Onboarding Experience

Getting set up is now smoother than ever. A guided flow walks you through choosing how you want to rename files—whether that's AI-powered Smart Rename, structured Templates, or freeform Custom Prompts.

Presets

Templates and Custom Prompts are now unified under Presets, with major upgrades to both:

**Templates** got a complete overhaul:

  • **Visual pattern builder** with drag-and-drop chips that show exactly how your filename will look
  • **Per-field separators** so you can mix dashes, underscores, and spaces however you need
  • **Live preview** updates as you build, so you always know what you're getting
  • **Test your templates** while building and iterate until they're perfect

**Custom Prompts** now have a cleaner, more prominent UI that makes them easier to create and manage.

Smarter Rules

The rules engine is now easier to use and more powerful:

  • **Card-based layout** makes it simple to see and manage your rules at a glance
  • **Archive and Trash actions** give you more options beyond just moving files
  • **Logic operators** let you combine conditions for precise control (AND/OR)
  • **Date conditions** for rules that trigger based on when files were created or modified

Refreshed Design

The whole app feels cleaner—modern glass-style buttons, smooth sidebar transitions, and polished drop zones throughout.
