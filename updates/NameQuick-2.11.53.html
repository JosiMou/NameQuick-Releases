Release date: 2026-06-28

This update improves checkout recovery and activation reliability for Self-Managed trials.

New

Improvements

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed Self-Managed trial activation getting stuck after checkout. NameQuick now recovers more reliably when checkout finishes but license confirmation takes longer than expected.
  • Fixed unclear recovery when activation cannot be confirmed. Settings now points you back to the right activation step instead of making a paid-but-stuck purchase look like a missing license key.
  • Fixed abandoned checkout sessions staying in progress. NameQuick now resolves canceled or unverifiable activation attempts more cleanly, so the app does not keep waiting on a checkout that is no longer active.
  • Fixed repeat activation retries after restart. Pending Self-Managed trial activation now resumes with better boundaries and stops stale retry loops when the license cannot be verified.
