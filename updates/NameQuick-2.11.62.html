New

Improvements

  • Clearer duplicate cleanup results. No-duplicates and completed-cleanup screens now present results and next actions more consistently, while partial cleanups still show the files that need attention.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed incorrect totals for similar images. Duplicate Detection now includes reviewed similar-image groups in its group and reclaimable-space totals.
