Release date: 2025-12-07

  • Fixed issue where duplicate files could incorrectly receive the same AI-generated name as a previously processed file.
  • Fixed template preview incorrectly showing "Could not extract" errors when fields were actually extracted successfully.
  • Fixed sidebar disappearing after closing Settings.
  • Added tooltips to sidebar tabs explaining their purpose (All Files, To Process, Processed).
  • Fixed files skipped by rename filters staying stuck in "To Process" instead of moving to "Processed".
  • Added "Remove from Library" option to remove files from the app without deleting them from disk.
  • Added "Clear All" button in the Processed tab toolbar to quickly remove all processed items from the library.
