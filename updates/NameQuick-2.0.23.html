v2.0.23 — Queue Management Made Smoother

### What’s New

  • New queue management popover – Introducing a dedicated queue popover where you can view all your queued rename files. Remove individual items or clear the entire queue with a single click—everything updates instantly with live badge counts.

Improvements & Fixes

  • Editing made stable – Fixed an issue where the app would crash when trying to edit your default naming template. You can now safely customize all your templates without any hiccups.
  • License management simplified – Resolved a quirk where the app would sometimes re-activate itself after you’d reset your license. Once you unlink your license, it stays unlinked until you choose to activate it again.
