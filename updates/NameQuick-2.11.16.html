Release date: 2026-04-05

New

  • License and access state now recover more cleanly after checkout, restore, and relaunch. NameQuick does a better job carrying your activation state forward without requiring extra cleanup from you.

Improvements

  • BYOK access feels more reliable across trial and paid states. The app now keeps the right access path and avoids drifting into the wrong upgrade or billing flow.
  • Onboarding progress stays in sync more consistently. Reopening the app is less likely to leave setup guidance out of step with where you actually are.
  • Quick actions and toolbar status feel steadier during a session. Popovers, action states, and startup UI now behave more predictably.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed launch and restore edge cases that could leave access in the wrong state. Licensing and app access now converge more reliably after startup.
  • Fixed trial and paid BYOK cases that could show the wrong state or charge target. Paid access is less likely to be overridden by stale trial state.
  • Fixed navigation and handoff issues around onboarding and activation. Follow-up guidance is less likely to appear at the wrong time or get stuck between steps.
