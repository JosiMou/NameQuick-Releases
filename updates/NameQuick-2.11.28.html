Release date: 2026-05-13

New

Improvements

  • Made saved templates more reliable. Your templates and custom prompts are now more likely to stay available after app updates, relaunches, or Finder Quick Action use.
  • Improved template syncing with Finder Quick Actions. Templates used from Finder now stay better aligned with the ones you saved in the app.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed the Automation permission row getting stuck. First-run users now see the Grant Access option when Automation permission has not been decided yet, and the row updates right after they respond.
  • Fixed a bug where templates and custom prompts could disappear. Saved naming templates and custom prompts should now remain available instead of unexpectedly resetting.
