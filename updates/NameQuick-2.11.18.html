Release date: 2026-04-19

New

  • Managed plan upgrades now feel more direct and clearer. When you run out of credits on a managed plan, NameQuick can guide you through upgrading with clearer billing choices and more localized pricing details.

Improvements

  • Account, license, and access state stay in sync more reliably. Startup, checkout, restore, and relaunch flows are less likely to leave the app showing the wrong access state.
  • BYOK and managed access paths are handled more consistently. NameQuick does a better job keeping trial, paid, and managed states on the right path instead of drifting into the wrong upgrade or billing flow.
  • Billing and upgrade screens are easier to understand. Pricing, confirmation, and plan selection around subscription changes now feel more explicit and less guessy.
  • Onboarding and license guidance feel steadier during setup. Status surfaces, follow-up prompts, and related UI handoffs behave more consistently as the app catches up to your real account state.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed cases where checkout or restore could leave access looking wrong until the app recovered. Licensing state now converges more reliably after post-purchase handoffs.
  • Fixed paid upgrade edge cases around managed subscriptions. Subscription changes are less likely to route through the wrong path or show incomplete billing context.
  • Fixed launch-time state mismatches between account identity, entitlement state, and visible UI. The app is less likely to land on the wrong surface or show stale licensing information on open.
  • Fixed a set of onboarding and popover timing issues. Guidance is less likely to appear out of step, get stuck, or point at the wrong moment during setup.
