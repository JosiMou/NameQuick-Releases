Release date: 2026-05-14

New

Improvements

  • Made subscription sign-in more reliable. NameQuick now handles more account details from the billing system, so active customers are less likely to be asked to start checkout again.
  • Improved account checks after checkout. Returning to the app after starting a managed AI trial now handles existing account access more carefully.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed a bug where active subscription trials could fail to verify. Customers with an active managed AI trial should now be recognized correctly instead of seeing a connection-style verification error.
  • Fixed a bug that could mix up managed AI and self-managed access. NameQuick now keeps self-managed provider choices separate from managed AI subscriptions more consistently.
