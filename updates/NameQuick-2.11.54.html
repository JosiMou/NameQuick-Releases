Release date: 2026-06-29

This update improves payment recovery for Managed subscriptions and makes template setup clearer.

New

Improvements

  • Clearer payment recovery for Managed subscriptions. If a Managed payment does not go through, NameQuick now shows an update-payment path in the app and Settings instead of sending you through a generic upgrade screen.
  • More responsive license refresh. The Refresh button in Settings now shows when NameQuick is checking your license, so it is clearer that your request is in progress.
  • Easier template setup. The template editor now makes it clearer that fields find data, while the filename builder controls what gets included in the final name.

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed failed Managed payments being treated like active access. NameQuick now blocks Managed features until billing is fixed, while keeping your account recoverable.
  • Fixed Unlock Full showing success before payment was confirmed. NameQuick now waits for the Managed payment to settle before ending the trial or showing full access.
  • Fixed failed Managed payments falling back to the wrong plan state. If your account also has Self-Managed trial benefits, NameQuick now guides you to fix billing for the Managed plan instead of showing a misleading trial or missing-license state.
  • Fixed payment recovery getting stuck after updating billing. NameQuick now rechecks your license when you return from the customer portal and offers a manual check-again option if the payment update needs a moment to settle.
